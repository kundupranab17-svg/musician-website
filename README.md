# Pobitra Kundu - Musician Website

This is the official portfolio website of **Pobitra Kundu**, a professional musician and sitarist.  
The website presents his musical journey, achievements, instruments he plays, and information for students who want to learn music from him.

## About the Musician
Pobitra Kundu is a passionate musician with more than **15 years of experience in music** and **5 years of teaching experience**. He is mainly a **sitarist** but is also skilled in playing multiple instruments including piano, tabla, flute, drums, and several types of guitar.

He secured **1st position in the national Kala Utsav competition**, performed in the prestigious **Dover Lane Music Conference competition**, and also won **1st place in railway music competitions**.

He is currently studying music at **Rabindra Bharati University** in West Bengal.

## Website Sections
The website includes the following sections:

- About the Musician
- Instruments Played
- Achievements
- Music Class Fees
- Photo Gallery
- Contact Information (Phone, Facebook, Instagram, WhatsApp)

## Purpose of the Website
The purpose of this website is to:
- Showcase Pobitra Kundu's musical profile
- Provide information about music classes
- Allow students to contact him for learning music
