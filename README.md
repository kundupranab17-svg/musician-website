# Pobitra Kundu – Music Classes Website

This repository contains the official website of **Pobitra Kundu**, a professional musician and sitarist. The website provides information about his musical journey, achievements, instruments played, music classes, and contact details for students interested in learning music.

## About the Musician

Pobitra Kundu is a passionate and dedicated professional musician with more than **15 years of experience in music**. He is primarily known as a **sitarist**, but he is also skilled in playing several other instruments including **tabla, piano, flute, drums, and several types of guitar**.

His musical journey began at a young age under the guidance of his first guru and father **Prasanta Kundu**. He later continued his training under **Niladri Kumar** and is currently guided by his main mentor **Dr. Rajib Chakraborty**.

## Achievements

Pobitra Kundu has received recognition in several prestigious competitions:

- **1st Position in National Kala Utsav Competition**
- Performed in the **Dover Lane Music Conference Competition**
- **1st Position in Railway Music Competitions**

These achievements highlight his dedication to classical music and his growing presence in the musical community.

## Teaching Experience

Apart from performing, Pobitra Kundu is also an enthusiastic teacher with **around 5 years of teaching experience**. He focuses on building strong musical fundamentals, proper technique, and creativity in his students.

He is currently continuing his musical education at **Rabindra Bharati University, West Bengal**, a well-known institution for arts and music education.

## Music Classes Offered

The following instruments are taught:

- **Sitar Classes** – ₹500 per class  
- **Tabla Classes** – ₹300 per class  
- **Piano Classes** – ₹300 per class
