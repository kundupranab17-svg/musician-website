About the Musician

Pobitra Kundu is a professional musician with 15 years of experience and 5 years of teaching experience. He is mainly a sitarist and has secured 1st position in National Kala Utsav and Railway music competitions. He has also performed in the Dover Lane Music Conference competition and is currently studying at Rabindra Bharati University.
