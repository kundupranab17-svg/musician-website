About the Musician

Pobitra Kundu is a professional musician with more than 15 years of experience in music and 5 years of teaching experience. He is mainly a sitarist and has secured 1st position in National Kala Utsav and Railway competitions. He has also performed at the Dover Lane Music Conference and is currently studying at Rabindra Bharati University.
